Banana Car

DESCRIPTION

Amazing banana car ready to take you on far journeys.

Propelled by human power, easy to maintain.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large banana
  • 2 rods
  • 4 wheels
  • Headlights and bumpers
  • Several strong humans

STEPS

  1. Perform perpendicular stabs with rods through banana.
  2. Attach wheels to rods.
  3. Carve out banana and put humans inside.
  4. Make holes so the human legs can touch the ground.
  5. Attach headlights and bumpers.
