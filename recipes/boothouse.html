Boot House

DESCRIPTION

Marvelous boot house, for the people on the go.

Paired with plains giants, this house can take you places from the comfort of your home.

INGREDIENTS

  • 420 bricks
  • windows
  • old boot
  • potion of growth
  • door

STEPS

  1. Put old boot on the ground and put potion of growth inside it.
  2. Unload bricks on the boot. Breaking the potion will activate boot growth and the bricks will stick to the boot.
  3. Add doors and windows.
  4. Paint to match your personality.
